Shiny App Demo

   Demo 1 - Portfolio management dashboard to visualize exposure metrics with capacities of drill-down (e.g., examining top drivers, historical trending) and period-over-period comparison to illustrate portfolio dynamics, credit rating migration, etc.
   Demo 2 - R Shiny app (implemented with modularization) for fitting ARIMA model for any financial time series obtained from Yahoo API. The app can be easily extended to multiple data sources (e.g., via Quandl) and data frequency.
   Demo 3 - R Shiny app (implemented with modularization) for illustrating PRA's PPNR/PBIL stress testing models based on BoE open data. The app can facilitate expert overrides on model specification and parameters. Furthermore, the app can easily accommodate bank's internal data.
   Demo 4 - A toy app (implemented in R markdown flexdashboard) for Economic Scenario Generator (ESG). The ESG is specified by a Vector Autoregression model built on the chosen macro time series.
